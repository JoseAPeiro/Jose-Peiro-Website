Croak Clash

Game Concept

In a swamp-themed world, there exists a multiplayer game featuring a humanoid frog character. Within this game, players have the freedom to select from various modes. The available minigames include "King of the Hill," "Flag Capture," and "Tag." In each minigame, the objective is to accumulate the highest number of points before time runs out, determining the winner. A leaderboard keeps track of the scores, and after three rounds of the minigame, the ultimate victor is announced.

My Participation

As the lead designer of this project, my main task was to keep a smooth flow of communication between the group. In addition, I was in charge of the documentation of the project, filling up the game design document (GDD), game mechanics, and level design. Furthermore, I was in contact with our client at the moment, Sickhouse, in Enschede, where the event took place.
