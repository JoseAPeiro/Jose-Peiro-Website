My Participation

Due to a NDA, I cannot showcase any of my work, however, I can describe my participation and collaboration at Liquid Ice Studios.

As the Game Design Intern, I was responsible for maintaining a smooth flow of communication between all team members, ensuring that everyone was aligned and informed throughout the project. My role involved a significant amount of documentation work, where I meticulously filled out the Game Design Document (GDD) to capture the project's vision, goals, and progress. I documented game mechanics in detail, providing clear guidelines and specifications for development. Additionally, I created HI-FI mock-ups for new game features, translating conceptual ideas into tangible designs that could be implemented by the development team.
